How I learn English as an interpreter

2019.02.19 Ruiqi

Ted talk

  • Listen, for the first time, notice where you don’t understand.
  • Check the transcript, understand the content & practicing saying that for a couple of times.
  • Summarize the logic behind that speech for speaking skills.
  • N.B.
    • listening capabilities, to different accents
    • content. knowledge.
    • logic. presentation skills. mindset.
    • speaking, useful expressions.

TV show

  • without subtitle, note where you don’t understand
  • with Eng subtitle, note where you don’t understand and check it out.
  • practice with it. including how it is said, expression, gesture and everything.
