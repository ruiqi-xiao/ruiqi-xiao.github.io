Where Are We Heading For?

2016.07.10 Ruiqi  热度 ℃

Recently, both me and Zen seemd to be strugging with the incentive to learn something new. He used to read technical books for a whole afternoon and never get bored, while he is easily getting sleepy when in library, now he is sleeping at the desk right in front of me :(

Where are we haeding for, a route to the loser’s house? How did it happen in the first place?

The main reason I believe is that the society, the place where we live is full of temptation. There are basically food available whenever you feel like chewing, sometimes you are not even starving. Roadside Stalls offer a wide range of street food from BBQ to stir fried noodles to fried chicken, all of which are tasety. I have to admit that the migrant workers from other small counties are really doing a good job(sad face). Besides midnight snacks, we also have cinema within walking distance. Given that Zen is a big movie fan, he checks the tickets frequently on his phone. You konw what will happen.

But it’s true that we have to shoulder parts of the responsibility here. It is pefectly understandable that one tends to jump in their comfort zone as soon as he get liberated form a long working day. But those who make a difference are always the ones that can commit themselve to a hard-working night(from 8-10pm), when one can be fully devoted to what interests him. Back to check what we usually do at that period, watching TV problem, having a quarrel, or eating junk food. Willpower is what we need most.

As is repeated around our ear along the way we grow up, where there is a will, there is a way. Hopefully, me and Zen can figure out where are heading for, what are we fighting for soon enough and get well prepared for whatever the future my have in store.
