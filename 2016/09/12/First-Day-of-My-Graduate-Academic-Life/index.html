First Day of My Graduate Academic Life

2016.09.12 Ruiqi  热度 ℃

Writing in English

How time flies! Two years have passed when I graduated from college. Earlier I happened to receive an email from my beloved college which I missed day and night for its yummy food! I did the test required in the email carefully in the hope that I can be of some help.

Kissing my college goodbye, I started my first lesson with a French class. The building, the libiary, the classroom, the way the teacher talks, everthing is so familiar but fresh. My memory back of my college life pumped out like a film right in front of me, about how I had spent all the precious youth from 18-22 years old. I couldn’t help but feel a little regret about the way I spent years before. Fortunately, I’ve got a second chance here in SUIBE with better academic atmosphere and platform. People don’t get a second chance easily. I swear I will spare no efforts this time to make it worthwhile.

Zen is preparing IELTS right now and I’m working on the Advanced Interpreting Test. Wish us all good luck and all our sweat.
